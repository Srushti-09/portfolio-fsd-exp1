01

Vaxi Babu

Research · UI/UX · Healthcare · Voice-First
Healthcare Innovation
Role
Research Lead & UI/UX Designer
Domain
Rural Healthcare
Key Focus
Voice-First Experience

The Challenge

Rural healthcare workers (ASHA workers) struggle with digital tools due to low literacy and limited smartphone familiarity. We needed a solution that felt natural and accessible — not another complex app.

Discovery & Research

Visiting ASHA workers in the field completely reshaped our approach. Through user interviews and contextual inquiry, we discovered that voice interaction was the most intuitive modality. This insight became the foundation of Vaxi Babu — a voice-first rural healthcare platform.

My Contribution

Led user research, designed the voice-first UX workflow, created the healthcare interface in collaboration with the Asha Foundation, designed pitch materials, and crafted the project's graphic identity.

Impact

Created a prototype that demonstrated how voice-first design can bridge the digital divide in healthcare. The project showed that empathy-driven research leads to fundamentally different — and better — solutions.

User Research User Interviews UI/UX Design Voice UI Healthcare Pitch Design Graphic Design
